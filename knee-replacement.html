What is Knee Replacement Surgery?

Knee replacement surgery, also known as “total knee replacement surgery” or “knee arthroplasty,” is a surgical procedure that removes the heavy surfaces of the knee joint to relieve pain and disability.

Anyone with severe arthritis or a knee injury may benefit from this surgery performed by the best knee replacement surgeon in Hyderabad.

How does arthritis affect the knee joint?

There are various types of arthritis that may affect the knee joint. Osteoarthritis is a degenerative joint disease that usually affects middle-aged and older adults. It can cause the breakdown of joint cartilage and adjacent bone in the knees. Rheumatoid arthritis can cause inflammation of the synovial membrane and result in excessive synovial fluid, leading to pain and stiffness. Traumatic arthritis may cause damage to the knee cartilage due to injury.

Common reasons for the knee replacement procedure

Knee replacement surgery is a treatment for knee pain and disability. The most common condition that results in the need for knee replacement surgery is osteoarthritis.

People with severe degenerative joint disease such as osteoarthritis may be unable to perform regular tasks that require knee bending, such as walking or climbing stairs, because they are painful. The knee may swell seeing that the joint is not stable.

Other types of arthritis, such as rheumatoid arthritis and arthritis caused by a knee injury, may also lead to knee joint degeneration. Furthermore, fractures, damaged cartilage, and/or torn ligaments might result in an irreversible knee joint injury.

When is it recommended?

Knee arthroplasty, often known as knee replacement, enhances a patient’s daily activities by relieving pain and discomfort. Everyone who has decided on knee replacement has the same question: when is the best time to have it done or when is it recommended?

Moreover, knee replacement surgery is recommended owing to several factors, including:

  • Stiff knees
  • Excessive swelling in the knees
  • Experiencing pain while walking, moving, or performing any physical movements
  • Extremely damaged knee joints or bones

Before recommending a knee replacement surgery, orthopedic doctors always prefer a thorough conversation with the patient.

The correct timing is one of the most important factors determining the success of knee replacement surgery. It indicates that having surgery at the right time benefits the patient the most.

Benefits of knee replacement surgery

Knee replacement surgery can benefit a patient in several ways, such as:

  • One of the major complaints with knee osteoarthritis is severe pain. Knee surgery can provide relief from chronic knee pain.
  • Knee surgery benefits you by reducing joint pain and stiffness, allowing you to go about your daily activities without pain.
  • Knee surgery decreases the requirement for long-term medicine, particularly opioid pain relievers and steroids. Long-term use of such medications can lead to a variety of complications.

There is no correct age at which one may need knee replacement surgery. After reviewing your symptoms and joint condition, an orthopedic surgeon may recommend knee replacement surgery. A knee replacement at the correct time may protect you from any future complications.
