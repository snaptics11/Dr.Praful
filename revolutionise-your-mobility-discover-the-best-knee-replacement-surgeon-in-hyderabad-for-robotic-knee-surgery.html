Revolutionise Your Mobility: Discover the Best Knee Replacement Surgeon in Hyderabad for Robotic Knee Surgery

Are you someone who has been suffering from knee pain for an extended period? Does your knee pain make it difficult for you to carry out daily activities? Explore the process and benefits of robotic knee replacement surgery and how to find the best knee replacement surgeon in Hyderabad for this specialized procedure.

What is Knee Replacement Surgery?

Knee replacement surgery is a medical procedure that involves removing damaged or diseased bone and cartilage from the knee joint and replacing it with an artificial joint made of metal, plastic, or ceramic. This procedure is typically performed on patients who have severe arthritis or who have experienced a knee injury.

The Evolution to Robotic Surgery

Technology has made it possible for surgeons to perform knee replacement surgery using a robotic-assisted process. Robotic knee replacement surgery involves using a robotic arm to assist the surgeon in removing the damaged bone and cartilage and placing the artificial joint.

During the surgery, the surgeon uses a computer to create a 3D model of the patient’s knee joint. The robotic arm is then programmed to remove the exact amount of bone and cartilage necessary to achieve the desired alignment and balance of the knee joint.

Benefits of Robotic Knee Replacement Surgery

  • Precision and Accuracy: Allows for more precise and accurate placement of the artificial joint, leading to better outcomes.
  • Less Invasive: Minimally invasive, reducing trauma to surrounding tissues and enabling faster recovery.
  • Customisation: Tailored procedure to match the patient’s specific anatomy for better fit and function.
  • Faster Recovery: Patients can return to normal activities sooner due to reduced surgical impact.

Finding the Best Knee Replacement Surgeon in Hyderabad

Choosing the right knee replacement surgeon is essential. Look for a surgeon with extensive experience, expertise in robotic knee replacement, and a strong track record of successful procedures.

Why Choose a Surgeon Who Specialises in Robotic Knee Replacement Surgery?

  • Precision and Accuracy: Specialised training ensures greater precision in surgery.
  • Innovation: Staying updated with the latest technology and techniques.
  • Experience: Higher volume of surgeries leading to better patient outcomes.

What to Expect During the Robotic Knee Replacement Surgery Process

  1. Preparation: The patient receives anesthesia, and the surgeon makes an incision to access the knee joint.
  2. 3D Mapping: A 3D model of the knee is created to guide precise surgical actions.
  3. Robotic-Assisted Surgery: The robotic arm assists in removing damaged bone and placing the artificial joint.
  4. Closing the Incision: The procedure is completed, and the incision is closed.

Recovery and Rehabilitation After Robotic Knee Replacement Surgery

Post-surgery, the patient typically stays in the hospital for a few days and undergoes physical therapy for rehabilitation. Most patients resume normal activities within 4-6 weeks, though full recovery may take up to 6 months.

Conclusion

Robotic knee replacement surgery offers several benefits over traditional methods, including greater precision, faster recovery, and improved patient outcomes.
